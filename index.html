About Us

The Innside Story is an Interior Design Company founded by Versha Gokulka in 2017. Their design philosophy is finding simplicity in clutter and to create in order to inspire.
The Innside Story’s goal is for its designs to have the power to transport you into another world, allow you to dream and most importantly, evoke an emotional response.
WE are happy to work on projects of any size from an individual room to a major re-development, from demolition & structural alterations and extensions to final decorations and furnishing. Our talented architectural and design team can transform any property into a beautiful space through architectural and interior design. From the minimalistic, luxurious and contemporary to the classic contemporary. Our TEAM transforms any new build or renovation into an award winning design for any taste.
Looking for a professional expert to shed some light on your outdated ineterior and turn it into one that you enjoy spending time in?
When you choose to work with us , we listen to your interior design requests and are fully committed to making your interior design dreams come true.
